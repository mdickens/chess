How to Play

This is a standard game of chess. The rules are the same as international chess.

Buttons

  • New Game: Starts a new game.
  • Flip Board: Flips the board to the other player's perspective.
  • Takeback: Takes back the last move.
  • Resign: Resigns the game.
  • Claim Draw: Claims a draw if the conditions are met. The primary condition is the fifty-move rule, where no pawn has been moved and no piece has been captured in the last fifty moves.
  • Hint: Shows a hint for the next move.
  • Help: Shows this help modal.
